Why Walnut Advisors

Bridging the execution gap

We identify solutions which are holistic in approach. Our past learning and experience suggest that great companies emerge when plans are executed perfectly, but business conditions always create challenges to detour our execution. We provide a helping hand to hold you though the crest and trough of economic cycles.

Data Centic Approach

Our approach is data led, we believe to empower decision of enterpreneurs backed by numbers and not their instincts alone. We capitalise on others learning experiences than reinventing the wheel. Our team creates mechanism to decipher collected data in useful information.

Partners

We are involved in the business as a partner and not an external advisor or consultant. We believe that without stepping in the shoes of an enterpreneur, the solutions offered are either impractical or temporary, which may instigate other issues to crop up.

Our Services

Assurance & Advisory

We offer an independent professional service, with the goal of presenting and interpreting collected data in holistic information, so that decision makers can make more informed, data represented decisions.

Financial Services

We help our clients through the all phases of a transaction, from getting industry insights, to preparing deck, to creating a financial model, to valuation assessment, to transaction due diligence and finally, implementation and closure of the transaction.

Financial Restructuring

For any business to turn successful, a business has to execute a series of ideas but not every idea is execuuted perfectly leading to stressed business condition. We offer services to enable our clients to minimise their risk and restrict their losses to address such unforseen situations

Corporate Finance Advisory

We work with a diverse set of clients to simplify all aspects of fundraising, right from early stage funding to growth to partial and complete exit. Our deep knowledge of the market and strong network enable us to identify a partner who is aligned with the organisation objective, vision and mission.
